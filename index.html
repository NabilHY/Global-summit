Real Madrid World Fans SUMMIT 2015

A joyful celebration believing in the value of sportsmanship and sharing, bringing in together more than 100 Real madrid fan clubs from all over the world, right here in the middle of Madrid right in the great santiago bernabeu stadium.

2015.07.07(FRI) ~ 16(SUN)

@ Stadium Santiago Bernabeu, Confrence Center and more

Main Program

Conferences

Listen to the speakers from various countries, with the prensence of the Club president.

Exhibition

Forum

Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with the club's chairmen for each topic.
